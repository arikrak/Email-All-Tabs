Email All Tabs - New Options

Email all tabs lets you email all tabs in a browser window. You have two options in version 0.8:

  • New option (recommended) - Opens new email and copies all tabs to clipboard. Paste the copied tabs into the email (with ctrl-V), and it will display the titles of each tab.
  • Legacy option - Automatically pastes the URLs into your email, but leaves out the titles (and sometimes some URLs).

Tips

  • Make sure to set your preferred email provider for email links, or this extension won't work (see instructions here).
  • Combine this extension with a simple tab manager, such as Tab Scissors so you can quickly choose which tabs to email.
  • Just want to save tabs without emailing anyone? You can save your email as a draft, or paste in the links elsewhere!
